Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Euchre account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app.

In the app (instant)

  1. Open Euchre and tap Play Online from the main menu.
  2. Tap your profile card at the top of the lobby.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account, then confirm.

Your account and all associated data are removed immediately and permanently. This cannot be undone.

By email

If you can’t access the app, email mickfulton@gmail.com from the address linked to your account and ask us to delete it. We’ll action the request and confirm.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following:

  • Your account and email address
  • Your profile — display name, avatar, XP and level
  • Your stats — games, wins, streaks and leaderboard entry
  • Your friends list and friend requests
  • Your match history

Data retained: none tied to your identity. Deletion is immediate and complete; there is no additional retention period. (Anonymous, non-identifying game records that never reference your account may remain.)
