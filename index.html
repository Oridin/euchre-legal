Privacy Policy

Euchrethe card game

Last updated 3 July 2026

Euchre is a card game published by Oridin. This policy explains what data the app handles and why — and we keep it minimal on purpose.

♠What we collect

You can play the entire offline game against the computer with no account and no data collection at all.

If you use the online features — Play Online, private rooms, friends, and the leaderboard — we collect:

  • Email address — only if you choose to sign in with email. You can play online as a guest with no email.
  • Account identifier — a random id for your account.
  • Profile & gameplay data — your display name, chosen avatar, XP, level, win/loss stats, friends list, and match history.

We do not collect your location, contacts, phone number, or advertising identifiers. The app contains no ads and no analytics or tracking.

♥How we use it

Solely to provide the online features: to sign you in, match you with other players, show your stats and leaderboard position, and let you add friends.

♣Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your display name, avatar, and public stats are visible to other players — on the leaderboard or across the table — which is simply how online play works.

♦Storage & security

Online data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) and sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

♠Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and everything tied to it at any time, right in the app:

Play Online → your profile → Delete account. This removes your profile, stats, friends, and match history, and cannot be undone.

You can also email us to request deletion (below).

♥Children

The app is intended for players aged 13 and older and is not directed at children under 13.

Questions or deletion requests: mickfulton@gmail.com

We may update this policy from time to time; the date at the top will change when we do.
